Our Story

About Bawadi

A Palestinian family-owned restaurant serving the DMV with Levantine and Mediterranean cooking, made the traditional way in Falls Church.

Rooted in Family

Recipes passed down, cooked from scratch

Bawadi Mediterranean Grill is a Palestinian family-owned restaurant serving heartfelt Levantine and Mediterranean cooking rooted in family recipes: slow-marinated kabobs, hand-rolled grape leaves, fresh-baked bread, and rice dishes simmered the traditional way.

Every dish starts with quality halal meats, fresh vegetables, and spices blended in-house, brought together in recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're stopping in for a quick shawarma or gathering the family around a mixed grill platter, our goal is the same: honest, flavorful food served with hospitality.

We're proud to be a neighborhood spot at 7 Corners in Falls Church, welcoming guests from across the DMV — dine in, carry out, or order online.
